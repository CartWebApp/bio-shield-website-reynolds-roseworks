Our Mission: Eco-Friendly Protection

Discover how Rey Roseworks creates natural bug repellents that protect people, pets, and the planet.

Who We Are

At Rey Roseworks, we believe that nature already provides the best solutions for protection and wellness. That’s why we’ve created the Bio Shield line—eco-friendly, plant-based sprays and essential oils designed to repel bugs effectively while keeping your environment safe and chemical-free.

Why Choose Natural Bug Repellents?

Unlike traditional repellents filled with harsh chemicals like DEET, our sprays use the natural power of plants. Ingredients such as eucalyptus, citronella, lavender, and citrus oils create a protective barrier against insects—without harming your health, pets, or the environment.

  • Non-toxic & safe for families
  • Biodegradable & eco-friendly
  • Gentle on skin, tough on bugs
  • Cruelty-free & sustainable sourcing

Environmental Commitment

We’re not just protecting people—we’re protecting the planet. Every bottle is made from recyclable materials, and we work with local suppliers to reduce our carbon footprint. Our products are crafted to leave behind nothing but freshness and peace of mind.

How Bio Shield Works

Our formula is powered by essential oils known for their insect-repelling properties. These natural extracts disrupt bugs’ sensory systems, keeping them away without chemicals that can contaminate soil, air, and water.

  • Eucalyptus Oil: Strong insect repellent, refreshing aroma
  • Citrus Oils: Natural bug deterrent with uplifting scent
  • Lavender: Repels mosquitoes while calming the senses
  • Cedarwood: Protects against ticks and other pests

Our Vision

We envision a future where families can enjoy the outdoors without fear of harmful chemicals or biting bugs. By choosing Rey Roseworks Bio Shield, you’re joining a movement toward cleaner, greener, and safer solutions for everyone.
